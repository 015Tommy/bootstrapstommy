Playable Characters

Choose your side and pick from a wide array of unique Plants and Zombies, each with their own abilities, playstyles, and variants.
Master your favorites and discover new ways to play in the battle for Suburbia!

Peashooter

A versatile offensive plant, the Peashooter excels at rapid-fire attacks and can deploy a Chili Bean Bomb for explosive damage.

Sunflower

The primary healer for the Plant team, the Sunflower provides crucial support with sunbeam attacks and healing abilities for allies.

Foot Soldier

A frontline assault zombie, the Foot Soldier is equipped with a Z-1 Assault Blaster and can use a Rocket Jump for mobility or a Stink Cloud for area denial.

All-Star

A heavy-hitting zombie, the All-Star uses a Football Cannon for sustained damage and can deploy Imp Punts for explosive attacks or a Tackle for close-range damage.
